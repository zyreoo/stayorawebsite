Hotel Management Software Built for Real Hospitality Teams

Running a hotel is complicated enough without fighting your software. Stayora is a cloud-based hotel management software for independent properties — the kind where the owner knows guests by name, the front desk person also handles check-outs, and nobody has time for a two-week onboarding process.

What is a hotel PMS — and why does your property need one?

A hotel property management system (PMS) is the software that stops everything from falling apart. Reservations, room status, check-ins, housekeeping — all in one place instead of scattered across notebooks, group chats, and institutional memory. Most traditional PMS systems were designed for large hotel chains: slow to set up, expensive to license, and built to be configured by a consultant rather than the person actually running the hotel. Stayora works differently. You set it up yourself, your staff figures it out the same day, and it doesn't cost more than a decent night's rate.

Built for boutique hotels, guesthouses, and independent properties

Enterprise PMS vendors aren't really built for a 20-room boutique hotel — and their pricing makes that clear. Stayora is built for independent properties: B&Bs, guesthouses, small urban hotels, family-run inns. Places where the owner is often also the front desk, the manager, and the one doing the end-of-day reconciliation. Good hotel operations software at this level should be practical, not a six-month implementation project. Plans start at $49/month, no consultant required.

Real-time booking management that eliminates overbookings

Every booking — phone, email, walk-in — lands on the same calendar the moment it's added. No manual syncing, no secondary spreadsheet to update. If the room is taken, it shows as taken. That's how overbookings stop happening.

Staff and housekeeping management in one platform

Most hotel booking management systems don't think much about the people making the beds. Stayora does. Shift schedules, cleaning tasks, and room status sit in the same dashboard as your reservations. So when a guest asks about early check-in, you know the answer without making three phone calls.

If you've been running your hotel on a mix of spreadsheets, WhatsApp threads, and memory — Stayora is the fix. It's a hotel management system that actually fits how independent properties work. Take a look at the pricing or get in touch to see how it works for your property.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Stayora and hotel management software.

What is hotel management software?

Hotel management software is what keeps a hotel running without constant manual coordination. It handles reservations, room availability, check-ins, check-outs, housekeeping, and staff — all in one place instead of scattered across notebooks, email threads, and someone's memory. You'll also hear it called a hotel PMS (Property Management System). The core idea is that everyone on your team sees the same information at the same time, so things don't fall through the cracks.

Is Stayora a property management system (PMS)?

Yes — it's a cloud-based hotel property management system (PMS) built for independent hotels. Reservations, room availability, check-ins, guest records — all the core stuff is there. It also handles staff coordination and housekeeping in the same system, which most traditional PMS vendors either don't do well or charge extra for.

Is Stayora suitable for small hotels and guesthouses?

That's the main use case, honestly. The Starter plan supports up to 15 rooms and is built around smaller independent properties — B&Bs, guesthouses, boutique hotels, small inns. Most PMS systems treat properties this size as an afterthought. Stayora doesn't. You can have the whole thing configured on day one, without a training session or a consultant.

How does Stayora prevent hotel overbookings?

Every booking, regardless of how it came in, goes into the same live calendar. There's no secondary spreadsheet to update, no message to send to the front desk. If a room is booked, it's marked as booked — for everyone on the team, instantly. Most double-bookings happen because one person took a phone reservation and another took a walk-in and nobody told each other. That doesn't happen when everyone's working from the same screen.

What is the difference between Stayora and a traditional hotel PMS?

Traditional PMS systems were designed for large hotel chains with IT departments and implementation budgets. They run on-premise, cost a lot to license, and typically need weeks of configuration before anyone can use them. Stayora is a cloud-based hotel management system that runs in any browser, starts at $49/month, and is the kind of software where you can figure out most of it on your own in the first day. Housekeeping and staff management are also built in — not sold as separate add-ons.

Can I manage hotel housekeeping and staff schedules with Stayora?

Yes, on the Pro and Enterprise plans. Shifts, task assignments, and room cleaning status are all in the same dashboard your front desk uses for bookings. So when a guest wants to check in early, you don't need to call anyone — you look at the screen and see whether the room is ready.

How does hotel booking management work in Stayora?

The booking management side of Stayora is a calendar where every room is a row and every reservation is a block on that row. You drag to move bookings, click to edit details, mark check-ins when guests arrive, and flag rooms for housekeeping when they check out. Any change your team makes shows up for everyone immediately — no syncing, no update messages, no calling the front desk.
